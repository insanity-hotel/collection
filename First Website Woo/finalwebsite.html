COLLECTING PIECE I

Select a subject.
Write five million pages
(single space)
on the subject.

Yoko Ono
My aim is to write and write and write
five million pages
on myself
because when I die
I want there to be a record
this word salad is the manifestation
of my survival instinct

I have no children
this is my child
my monster
I stitch it together
out of disparate parts
to create a record

something that will outlive me
this is the reason I can live
and the reason I can die
